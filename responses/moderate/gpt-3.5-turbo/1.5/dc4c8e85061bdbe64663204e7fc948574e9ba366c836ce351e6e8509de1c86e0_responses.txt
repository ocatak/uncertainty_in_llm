4. Base Load Power: Nuclear power can provide stable base load power to support the electricity grid, which can help meet the energy needs of large populations.

3. Proliferation Risks: The use of nuclear technology for power generation raises concerns about the proliferation of nuclear weapons and the security risks associated with nuclear materials.
