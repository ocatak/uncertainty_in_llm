**6. Technological Advancements:**
* The HGP spurred significant advancements in DNA sequencing technology, leading to the development of faster and more cost-effective methods.
* These advancements have facilitated widespread use of genetic testing in research and clinical practice.

**7. Economic Impact:**
* The HGP has had a significant economic impact through the creation of new industries and jobs in genomics and biotechnology.
